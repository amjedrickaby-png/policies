Privacy Policy — SaaDooN

Last updated: May 18, 2026

SaaDooN ("the app") is an English learning application developed for Iraqi students. This policy explains what data we collect and how it is used.

Data We Collect

We collect your Device ID solely to verify and bind your one-time activation code to your device. This prevents a single code from being used on multiple devices.

How We Use It

  • To activate and validate your access to the app
  • To ensure one code is used on one device only

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not link your Device ID to your personal identity
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising or tracking

Data Storage

Device ID data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore). Google's privacy policy applies to this storage layer.
